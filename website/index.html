Why does this project exist?
On March 31, 2026, ~100 Baidu robotaxis simultaneously shut down on elevated highways in Wuhan. Passengers were trapped for 2 hours. SOS buttons went unanswered. Resolution: police officers rescuing vehicles one by one. This is not sustainable at scale.

Reference Architecture: Three-Layer Decision Model

Layer 1: AI Autonomous Response

70%

AI identifies the scenario, assesses risk, generates a response plan, and executes autonomously. Examples: pull over safely, reroute around obstacle, restart perception module, calm passenger via voice.

Layer 2: AI-Assisted + Human Confirm

25%

AI generates a recommended action plan with confidence score. Remote operator reviews, confirms or modifies, then the vehicle executes. Examples: complex intersection escape, police gesture interpretation, multi-vehicle conflict resolution.

Layer 3: Remote Driving / On-Site Dispatch

5%

Full remote teleoperation at ≤10 km/h, or dispatch on-site rescue team. Examples: vehicle hardware failure (cannot self-move), accident scene management, vehicle fire, mass system failure.
